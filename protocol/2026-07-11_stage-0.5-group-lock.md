## Repeated-subject structure

- 75 unique source subjects across 108 specimens.
- 23 subjects contributed more than one specimen.
- 14 late-wound, 11 early-wound and 2 HTS specimens have a normal-skin specimen from the same source subject.

Primary models will account for subject correlation where feasible. A matched/within-subject sensitivity analysis will be carried out only in the available paired subsets. It will not be presented as a full longitudinal analysis because the paired structure is incomplete and scar samples are usually from separate later contexts.
